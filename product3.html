Amaron, under the umbrella of Amara Raja Energy & Mobility Ltd., is one of India's most trusted battery manufacturers. Known for their reliability and performance, Amaron Quanta batteries are widely used in UPS systems across various industries, ensuring uninterrupted power supply in critical applications.

1. Amaron Quanta SMF VRLA Batteries

Amaron Quanta SMF VRLA batteries are designed for high-performance applications, providing reliable power backup in UPS systems. These batteries are known for their maintenance-free operation and long service life, making them ideal for use in data centers, healthcare facilities, and other critical infrastructure.

Key Features:
  • Maintenance-free design with advanced AGM technology
  • High durability with reinforced end walls for impact resistance
  • Low self-discharge rate for extended shelf life
  • Proven performance in Indian conditions
  • Available in various capacities to suit different applications
